50 Years

This selection of 50 images, objects, letters, postcards and photographs from the archives of the Lisson Gallery celebrates the fiftieth anniversary of its founding in 1967. This changing digital platform represents just a fraction of the gallery’s extensive archive and its 50th anniversary publication, ARTIST | WORK | LISSON, which documents over 500 exhibitions and solo shows by more than 150 artists.

Also marking the gallery’s half-century in 2017 is ‘EVERYTHING AT ONCE’, an ambitious exhibition that condenses key aspects of Lisson Gallery’s past, present and future. Produced in collaboration with The Vinyl Factory, the exhibition features a range of historical and recent works by a number of gallery artists in a variety of media.
