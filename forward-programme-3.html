Haroon Mirza
67 Lisson Street, 24 February – 8 April

Haroon Mirza presents a new exhibition forming a constellation of related installations that incorporate light, sound and sculptural works, as well as a living ecosystem based around an ant farm. In the main room, Mirza presents a new work in his series of ‘modular operas’ – a malleable system of interconnected video and performance works – in which a tea ceremony is conducted using the active ingredient of the fly agaric mushroom. Surrounding and enveloping the viewer is the sound of an electro-acoustic gong bath performed by Mirza, based around the so-called ‘Holy’ or ‘Divine’ frequency of 111Hz, which is purported to have healing or mind-altering properties.
